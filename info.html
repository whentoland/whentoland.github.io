WhenToLand - Land time voltage calculator

WhenToLand calculates the theoretical voltage, amps and time at which you need to start landing


The results shown are based on average performance of a variety of typical RC batteries. However note that each battery and each setup behaves differently so use these estimates as guidance and then fine tune them on your setup.

A couple of key assumptions:
  • If you enter a drain of over 25C, for calculation purposes it is assumed that the drain is 25C
  • The voltage drop of LiPos under load at different C levels as well as the percentage capacity in relation to voltage has been calculated as an average from a dozen online sources
  • It is assumed that its time to land at when the LiPo has 25% of its Amps left
